# Publisher-Grade Final Review Checklist

## Text

- The translation is faithful to the source meaning and natural in the target language, without obvious translationese.
- Numbers, dates, proportions, names, quotations, and page references are correct.
- Terms, proper names, forms of address, and chapter titles are consistent across the book.
- No OCR remnants remain: broken words, broken URLs, incorrect fractions, headers/footers, or page numbers mixed into the text.

## Structure

- Chapter order, heading hierarchy, table of contents, spine, and nav match the original book.
- Preface, appendices, endnotes, bibliography, and index have not been omitted.
- Working-draft page comments have not leaked into the final EPUB.

## Figures and Tables

- No figures, tables, formulas, or illustrations are missing.
- Cropped images include only the target content; they are not whole-page screenshots and do not include surrounding body prose.
- Cropped images are not blank, cut off, from the wrong page, or padded with excessive whitespace.
- The body text does not contain duplicate or broken OCR table remnants.

## Notes

- Notes are close to the corresponding source text, or have reliable bidirectional links.
- `*`, `†`, `‡`, and numbered notes have not become list items or ordinary paragraphs.
- Decimals, years, and numbered items have not been misclassified as notes.
- Note order matches the original book.

## EPUB

- EPUBCheck has no errors and preferably no warnings.
- Images are included in the OPF manifest and resource paths are valid.
- XHTML parses correctly and ZIP integrity passes.
- Paragraphs, figures, tables, and notes remain readable on mobile or narrow screens.
